Lore & Theories

Pharloom's History

Pharloom was once a thriving kingdom ruled by the Silk Weavers, masters of the ancient art of silk manipulation. The kingdom fell into ruin when the Silk Song, a powerful artifact, was corrupted by dark forces.

Hornet's True Identity
Many believe Hornet is the last descendant of the Silk Weavers, explaining her natural affinity for silk manipulation and her role as Pharloom's protector.
The Silk Song's Purpose
The Silk Song may have been created to maintain the balance between light and shadow in Pharloom, but its corruption led to the kingdom's downfall.
Lace's Connection
Lace appears to be a corrupted Silk Weaver, transformed by the dark forces that corrupted the Silk Song. Her attacks mirror the ancient silk techniques.
