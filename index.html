Game Description

The deadly hunter Hornet finds herself alone in a vast and strange kingdom. She must fight enemies, seek allies, and solve puzzles on her deadly pilgrimage to the kingdom's peak.

Hornet must master a brand new set of powerful moves to survive. She will unleash devastating attacks, learn incredible silk abilities, and craft deadly tools to overcome the kingdom's challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Hollow Knight: Silksong

When will the game be released?

Confirmed at the Nintendo Direct on April 2, 2025, the game will be released on September 4, 2025. Team Cherry is working hard to perfect the game and ensure the best experience for players.

Do I need to play the original Hollow Knight first?

While not mandatory, it is highly recommended to experience the original game first to better understand the game world and character backgrounds. Silksong is Hornet's independent adventure, but has deep connections with the original game.

What platforms will the game support?

The game will support Windows, Mac, Linux and Nintendo Switch. Other platforms like PlayStation and Xbox may be announced later. The game will also support Xbox Game Pass.

How long is the game?

According to Team Cherry's estimate, the game length is about 15-20 hours, depending on the player's exploration level and skill level. This is slightly shorter than the original game, but the content is more compact.

What new abilities does Hornet have?

Hornet has a brand new movement system including jumping, gliding and a series of weapon skills. She can also use silk abilities to create platforms, attack enemies and solve puzzles.

Will there be DLC for the game?

Team Cherry has not announced DLC plans yet, but considering the free DLC support for the original game, Silksong is likely to have additional content updates as well.
