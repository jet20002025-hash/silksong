Hollow Knight: Silksong Officially Releases September 4, 2025!

Team Cherry officially confirmed at the Nintendo Direct on April 2, 2025, that Hollow Knight: Silksong will be released on September 4, 2025. This highly anticipated sequel will bring players a brand new adventure experience as Hornet embarks on an epic journey in the kingdom of Pharloom. The game will be available on Nintendo Switch, PC, PlayStation and Xbox platforms.
