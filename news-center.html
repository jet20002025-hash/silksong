Official Announcement January 15, 2025

Hollow Knight: Silksong Release Date Confirmed - September 4, 2025!

Team Cherry has officially announced that Hollow Knight: Silksong will launch on September 4, 2025, across all major platforms including Nintendo Switch, PC (Steam), PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S. The announcement came during Nintendo's April Direct presentation, where fans were treated to a brand new gameplay trailer showcasing Hornet's silk-based abilities, new areas of Pharloom, and several previously unseen boss encounters. Pre-orders are now available on all platforms, with special collector's editions featuring exclusive artwork, soundtrack, and physical merchandise. The game will support multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, and Chinese. Key Features Confirmed:

  • Over 150 new enemies and 15+ boss encounters
  • Expanded Pharloom kingdom with interconnected areas
  • New silk-based combat and traversal mechanics
  • Multiple endings based on player choices
  • Co-op mode for up to 2 players
